MindCare Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MindCare. Your privacy and trust are important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, password, role (user/counselor/admin), gender, and phone number when you register.
  • Usage Data: Information about your activity on MindCare, including mood logs, reminders, sessions, and interactions with counselors or the community.
  • Technical Data: Device, browser, operating system, IP address, and cookies for security and analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize our services, including mood tracking, reminders, and counseling sessions.
  • To communicate with you about your account or platform updates.
  • To improve MindCare’s features, security, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our users and platform.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share data with counselors/admins for platform functions.
  • We may share information with service providers helping us operate MindCare (e.g., email, analytics) but only as needed and with strict confidentiality.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect safety and rights.

5. Data Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data.
  • Passwords are encrypted. Sensitive information is protected in transit and at rest.
  • Despite our efforts, no system can be 100% secure. Use strong passwords and notify us of any suspicious activity.

6. Your Rights

  • You may access, update, or delete your personal information at any time via your profile settings.
  • You can request data deletion or account deactivation by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

7. Children’s Privacy

MindCare is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn we have done so, we will promptly delete such information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes via email or on our platform. Continued use of MindCare means you accept the new policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, contact us at support@mindcare.com
